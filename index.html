Why Korea?

My interest in Korea began with learning the Korean language, but over time I discovered something more meaningful. Through education, I realized how a single opportunity can completely change a person's future.

Studying Korean opened new doors for me, gave me confidence, and connected me with people and experiences that I never imagined before.

I want to study Computer Science in South Korea because I admire Korea's commitment to innovation, technology, and education. My goal is not only to develop technical skills, but also to create technologies that help others access educational opportunities just as I did.
